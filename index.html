How we help

We Represent and Provide an Exclusive Suite of Services to only the Top 100 CEOs.

Directorship

Board Career Management, where our Top 100 CEOs select and are named to their ideal preferred Directorships. Because of our unique access to, and relationships with, the best leaders and Directors, the Top 100 can select their ideal new Directors to their Boards.

Your Directors

We only present candidates from the top 100 and next generation 200 that meet our extreme leadership requirements. Providing the best CEO careers allowing for significant wealth creation including Backable CEO Private Equity and major consolidation play opportunities.

Brand Services

CEO Concierge offers premium services including personal brand enhancement/repair, unique access to targeted aquisitions, confidential introductions and secure arrangements to discuss any matters of sensitive strategic value.

What We have Done for CEOs

  • Our Leadership

    Our team is led by Jeff Christian who has conducted some of the most compelling CEO and Board searches during a very unique window in time. His experiences have allowed him a large enough sampling of leading CEOs to recognize remarkably similar characteristics, behaviors, and leadership qualities of the most exceptional high performance leaders. Based on his analysis we have created the only assessment tool to accurately identify our first grouping of Top 100 CEOs and Next Generation CEOs.


  • Past Successes

    We have secured key top 100 CEOs that drove signifcant growth for companies including Hewlett-Packard, Apple, Hyperion, F5 networks, and Chipcom (aquired by 3Com). In total, our leaders have helped execute over 100 major turnarounds and restarts, and created over 500 Billion dollars in asset improvement through securing a top 100 CEO.
